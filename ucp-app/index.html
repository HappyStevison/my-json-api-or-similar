Based on the teachings of Konchok Penday

How to Practice UCP

UCP (Universal Conscious Practice) is a powerful method for enhancing self-awareness and personal growth. Follow these steps:

  1. WHERE ARE YOU NOW? - Describe your current situation in detail.
  2. WHERE MIGHT YOU BE? - Imagine a possible future scenario.
  3. COMPARE THAT TO NOW. - Compare the future scenario to your present.
  4. WHERE HAVE YOU BEEN? - Reflect on a past experience.
  5. COMPARE THAT TO NOW. - Compare the past experience to your present.

Repeat the cycle until you feel complete. End the session when ready.

Benefits of UCP

  • Enhances self-awareness and clarity.
  • Helps resolve past traumas and emotional blocks.
  • Encourages creative thinking about the future.
  • Promotes a deeper understanding of your present reality.
